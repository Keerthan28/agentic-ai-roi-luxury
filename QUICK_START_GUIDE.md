## 📖 Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Home Input 📌

**What to do:**
1. Open the dashboard in your browser
2. In the left sidebar, you'll see organized parameter groups in expandable sections
3. On the main page, select your customer profiles (1-3 recommended)
4. Select your business pain points (1-3 recommended)
5. Enter your annual revenue
6. Click the red "🚀 Run Analysis" button

**What to look for:**
- ✓ badges showing how many items you've selected
- Helpful tooltips when you hover over parameters
- Expandable "How to Use" section for guidance

### Step 2: Customer Segmentation 📊

**What you'll see:**
- Your analysis scope with confirmed dataset size
- Segment overview cards showing customer distribution
- 2D PCA visualization showing how customers cluster
- Normalized radar chart comparing segments
- Detailed segment profiles with metrics
- Profile heatmap showing strengths/weaknesses
- Customer value vs. loyalty matrix

**Key metrics:**
- Segment size (# of customers and %)
- Average spending, CLV, and engagement
- Retention rates and digital propensity

### Step 3: Agentic AI Use Cases 🤖

**What you'll see:**
- Matched use cases for your business challenges
- Impact metrics for each use case
- Target segments affected
- Financial projections
- Strategic implementation details
- Success metrics and KPIs

**Key information:**
- Revenue uplift percentages
- Labor savings opportunities
- Total annual impact
- ROI contribution estimates

### Step 4: ROI Calculator 💰

**What you'll see:**
- Key financial metrics in styled cards:
  - Revenue Lift

- Detailed financial summary

**Key takeaways:**
- Expected payback period (usually 3-6 months)

### Step 5: Implementation Roadmap 🗺️

**What you'll see:**

- For each phase:

**Implementation timeline:**

## 📊 Understanding the Visualizations

### PCA Scatter Plot
- **Axes**: Principal components (primary variation dimensions)
- **Bubble Size**: Customer lifetime value (larger = higher value)
- **Colors**: Different customer segments
- **What to look for**: Distance between clusters, outliers

### Radar Chart
- **Each point**: A customer segment
- **Each axis**: A customer metric (normalized 0-1)
- **Larger polygon**: Better performance on that metric
- **Overlap**: Suggests similar segments

### Heatmap
- **Rows**: Customer segments
- **Columns**: Key metrics
- **Dark red**: Lower values
- **Yellow**: Medium values
- **Dark green**: Higher values

### 3-Year Projection
- **Green bars**: Revenue growth year-over-year
- **Blue bars**: Operating cost savings
- **Red line**: Total net benefit trending
- **Intersection point**: Shows payback period

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Analysis takes too long
- This is normal for 1000+ customer records
- Typical runtime: 15-30 seconds
- Watch the loading spinner

### No use cases matched
- Try selecting different pain points
- Fewer customer profiles often work better
- Check your pain point selections

### Metrics look wrong
- Verify your annual revenue input
- Check your customer selections
- Try re-running from scratch

### Charts not loading
- Refresh the browser
- Check your internet connection
- Clear browser cache
