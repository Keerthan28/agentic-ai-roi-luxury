## Third-Party Data

All OSINT benchmarks used in the ROI model are drawn from publicly available industry reports:

- Bain & Company — Luxury Market Study
- McKinsey — Luxury Report / Personalization research
- Deloitte — Global Powers of Luxury Goods
- BCG × Altagamma — True-Luxury Global Consumer Insight

These are cited in-line in `docs/methodology.md` and in the final report. No proprietary data or models from any of these firms is embedded in this repository; only the headline benchmark values are used.

## Survey Data

The "Luxury Goods Shopping Experience" survey was administered through Qualtrics. Respondents were anonymous; no direct identifiers are present in the export. Aggregate results and visualizations derived from the survey are safe to share; per-row responses are not redistributed.
